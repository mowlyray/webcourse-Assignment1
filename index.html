Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating, developing, and implementing new ideas, methods, or products to improve existing systems or solve problems in novel ways.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the fundamental facilities and systems that support the functioning of a society, economy, and community.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of enabling individuals or groups to gain control over their lives, make informed decisions, and achieve their full potential.

Vision Of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Yunus envisions a future where poverty is relegated to museums—a distant memory of a problem humanity once faced but has now overcome. Through the groundbreaking concept of microfinance and the establishment of Grameen Bank, Dr. Yunus demonstrated how access to credit can empower the poorest members of society, especially women, to break free from the cycle of poverty. His vision is rooted in the belief that every human being, regardless of their circumstances, has unlimited potential.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus
Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
